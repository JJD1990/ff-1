Fara Fonseca

Farah founded Fonseca Fitness in 2012 and is our head coach Farah has worked with thousands of women to help them to become the best version of themselves. In 2015 Farah studied at the College of Naturopathic Medicine and then after that started working with holistic coaches in America to learn more than what was covered with just a ‘basic’ personal training qualification. Farah has completed a course at the National Institute for Eating Disorders to help women and young girls address their relationship with both themselves, others and food. I also have a few sporting achievements under my belt (quite literally).

  • 3 x England’s Strongest Woman
  • 1 x Britain’s Strongest Woman
  • 2018 Strongest Woman in the World
  • Professional Strongwoman
For those who are ready to work hard, I am here for you. Not only for your time in the studio, but in all areas of your health journey.
